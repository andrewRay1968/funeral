In Loving Memory of
Andrew James Ray

1968 – 2026

It is with great sadness that we announce the passing of Andrew Ray. Andrew was a much-loved son, father, brother and friend whose kindness, humour and generosity touched the lives of so many.

We warmly invite all who knew Andrew to join us in celebrating his life and saying our final farewell. Your presence and support will mean a great deal to our family during this difficult time.
